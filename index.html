📌 Quick mental health toolkit

  • 🔹 Box breathing – Inhale 4s, hold 4s, exhale 4s, hold 4s. Calms the vagus nerve.
  • 🔹 5-4-3-2-1 grounding – Name 5 things you see, 4 you feel, 3 you hear, 2 you smell, 1 you taste.
  • 🔹 Self-compassion break – “This is hard. May I be kind to myself.”
  • 🔹 Limit social comparison – unfollow accounts that drain your energy, follow inspiring mental health advocates.
